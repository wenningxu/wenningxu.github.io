Wenning Xu

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Glasgow. My research focuses on human motion synthesis, character animation, and human-human interactions.

Previously, I completed an MSc in Artificial Intelligence (Distinction) at the University of Manchester, and a BSc in Computer Science at the University of Liverpool and Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University.
